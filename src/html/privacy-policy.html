Privacy Policy

At ClarityTech, we prioritize your privacy and data protection. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or use any of our digital services. By accessing this website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect both personal and technical data to help us provide a secure, responsive, and personalized experience. This may include:

  • Basic contact details like your name, email address, company name, and role—especially when you submit a form or contact us directly.
  • Technical data such as your device type, browser, IP address, and session duration for analytical and diagnostic purposes.
  • Behavioral data on how you interact with our content and features, helping us optimize usability.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance performance and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

2. How Your Information Is Used

The data we collect enables us to:

  • Respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, or deliver requested resources and updates.
  • Improve our website functionality, security, and the relevance of our services.
  • Understand visitor behavior and preferences to inform future improvements.
  • Comply with legal obligations and support accountability in our business operations.

Your data is used strictly for internal purposes and never sold or shared for advertising.

3. Data Protection and Security

ClarityTech employs strict measures to ensure your data remains secure. All information is transmitted via encrypted channels, stored securely, and accessible only to authorized personnel with a legitimate purpose.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and authorities in accordance with legal requirements.

4. Sharing of Data

We only share personal data when it is essential, such as:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email platforms) under confidentiality agreements.
  • With government agencies or regulators if required by law.

We never share your data for third-party marketing or unrelated purposes.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your personal data. At any time, you may request to:

  • Access the data we have about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your personal data or restrict its processing.
  • Withdraw consent or object to how your data is used.

To make a request, please email us at support@claritytech.co.

6. Email Communication

If you’ve subscribed to updates or resources, we may occasionally send you relevant content. You can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

7. Compliance with Legal Standards

We adhere to the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and other international data privacy frameworks as applicable. Our team continuously reviews internal processes to remain compliant with evolving laws and best practices.

8. Contacting ClarityTech

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, or if you’d like to exercise your data rights, we encourage you to contact us at: support@claritytech.co.

We are committed to responding promptly and transparently.
